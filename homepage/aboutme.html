Who Am I?

My name is Pedro and I was born on May 29, 2002 in Las Palmas de Gran Canaria, Spain. I have always been passionate about technology and computers, so I decided to pursue a degree in Computer Science.

I started my studies at the University of Las Palmas de Gran Canaria in 2020 and have enjoyed every moment of it. I have learned a lot about programming, algorithms, data structures, and computer systems. I have also had the opportunity to work on several projects, such as a web application for a local NGO and a mobile game for a startup.

In my free time, I like to keep up with the latest trends in technology and learn new programming languages. I also enjoy traveling and exploring new places. I have been to several European countries and hope to visit many more in the future.

Overall, I am very happy with my decision to study Computer Science and I am looking forward to a rewarding career in the field. I believe that my passion for technology and my dedication to learning will help me succeed in my chosen field.
